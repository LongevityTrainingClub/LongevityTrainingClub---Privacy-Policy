
# Longevity Training Club – Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** August 12, 2025  

Welcome to **Longevity Training Club**!  
Your privacy matters to us, so here’s a clear explanation of what we do (and don’t do) with your information.  

---

## 1. Information We Collect
When you use Longevity Training Club, we collect:  
- **Name** – from your Google account when you sign in.  
- **Email Address** – also from your Google account when you sign in.  

That’s it. We do not collect payment information, location data, or any other personal details at this time.  

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use your name and email to:  
- Create and manage your account.  
- Authenticate you when you log in.  
- Provide access to programs, articles, and updates in the app.  

We **do not** sell your information, use it for marketing, or share it with advertisers.  

---

## 3. How We Store and Protect Your Data
Your name and email are stored securely in **Supabase**. We take reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.  

---

## 4. Who We Share Your Data With
We only share your data with:  
- **Supabase** – for account authentication and secure storage.  
- **Legal authorities** – only if required by law.  

---

## 5. Children’s Privacy
The app is suitable for users **aged 9 and older**. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 9.  

---

## 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we do, we’ll post the updated version here with a new “Last Updated” date.
